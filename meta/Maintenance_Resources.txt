## PERFORMANCE THEORY & TUNING

Note: R1150RT uses Bosch Motronic fuel injection. Tuning options are limited 
compared to earlier models. These resources provide foundational understanding 
of engine performance principles.

Compression & Engine Theory
- Measuring Compression Ratio: http://www.cafebeemer.com.au/blog/Compression_ratio/compression_ratio.html
  (Useful for understanding compression testing results and engine health assessment)

Performance Theory
- High Performance Tuning Theory (Dr. Curve): http://garnet.acns.fsu.edu/~jroche/
  (Academic approach to engine performance - theory applicable across platforms, 
  though R1150RT ECU limits practical modifications)
